    Tips & tricks practical best practices
    • Group thirsty plants together so you can check them in one pass and spot stress faster.
    • When in doubt, under‑water snake plants and pothos; over‑watering kills them quicker than drought.
    • Stick to a small set of pot sizes and one base mix so repotting is predictable and fast.
    • If you move a plant, note it in that day’s checklist; it makes future troubleshooting actually useful.
    • If the same plant gets repeated “area of improvement” notes, change its location or profile, not just watering.
